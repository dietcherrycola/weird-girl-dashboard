🌿 Some supplements are most effective when cycled with your phases. All have research support for PCOS, PMDD, or both. Always check with your doctor.

Understanding your PMDD
PMDD is a real, serious neurological condition — not just "bad PMS." Your brain is more sensitive to the progesterone withdrawal after ovulation than most people's. Serotonin, GABA, and dopamine are all disrupted simultaneously. With PCOS, your already-fluctuating hormones make this more intense. This is biology, not a character flaw.
